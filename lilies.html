A Little about Lilies

These flowers are stunning. They come in a variety of shapes, sizes, fragrances and colors. They are categorized into various species.

Lilies can be given for so many different occasions.

They can be grown from bulbs or seeds. Growing them by bulb is the preferred method because growing them by seed take longer for the plants to grow.

It can take anywhere from 5 to 6 years for a Lily plant to grow and flower when culturing them by seed.

The Lily above is called a "Stargazer". This Lily can be one of the most fragrant of its kind. It is so fragrant;one plant can permeate a single room!
